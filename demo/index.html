Our Mission

At FoodBridge, our mission is to connect local restaurants with people in need by redistributing surplus food. Every day, restaurants waste a significant amount of food that could be used to feed the hungry, and we're here to change that.

How It Works

When restaurants donate their untouched, unused food to FoodBridge, they not only help feed the community but also unlock rewards through our Clean & Green Program. These rewards include essential services such as waste management and professional deep cleaning, ensuring your establishment stays spotless. Giving back has never been more rewarding!

Supporting Small Businesses

FoodBridge creates a win-win by helping restaurants reduce food waste while feeding those in need. Every donation strengthens the community and supports sustainability, turning surplus into sustenance. Together, we build a cleaner environment and a stronger, more connected society!
