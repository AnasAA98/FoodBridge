Food Bridge.

Connecting local restaurants to those in need.

Our Mission

At FoodBridge, our mission is to connect local restaurants with people in need by redistributing surplus food. Every day, restaurants waste a significant amount of food that could be used to feed the hungry, and we're here to change that.

Our Commitment to Charity

We believe no food should go to waste while so many people struggle with hunger. Our commitment to charity drives us to ensure that excess food from restaurants is rerouted to those who need it most, creating a more sustainable and compassionate community.

Supporting Small Businesses

FoodBridge also empowers small businesses to make a difference. By donating their unsold food, restaurants not only help reduce waste but also support those in their local communities who need it, all while enhancing their social impact.
